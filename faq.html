Weddings

Wedding Styling

Wedding styling includes groom and groomsmen suit coordination, color and fabric consultation, accessory guidance (ties, pocket squares, shoes), and ensuring a cohesive aesthetic across the entire wedding party. I handle the details so you can focus on enjoying your day.

I recommend booking at least 3–6 months before your wedding date to allow enough time for consultations, fabric sourcing, fittings, and any alterations. For larger wedding parties, earlier is better. That said, I always try to accommodate tighter timelines when possible.
